Kamie is a storytelling, code-enthusiast creative working towards making unique audio visual experiences.

Kamie’s first love was music. Quitting piano lessons in suburban Utah propelled her into it. Practicing was no longer about playing to someone else’s perfection. Expressing and creating was the captivating gem. Talking and jamming led to being a guest artist on Never Stop Failing’s song, 11 Months, that was featured in the hit XBOX Live Arcade game Shadow Complex, and playing at Guitars Unplugged and open mic events.

Desiring to extend her storytelling passion visually, she was accepted at the Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA) to study Photographic and Electronic Media.

Kamie lives in the LA area and continues to pursue merging audio, visual and digital components to explore concepts and tell stories. She is studying at The Songwriting School of Los Angeles in parallel to learning JavaScript.
