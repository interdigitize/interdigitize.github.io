A Mural to Brighten the Child Development Center at St. Vincent Hospital

Sep 14, 2011   |  Tags: photography

I traveled with the artists and documented and summarized the exchange. Read the summary article.



The director of St. Vincent de Paul Hospital, Dr. Salim Nakhleh, proposed the mural to forPEACE director Margret Ellwanger in October 2010.




The forPEACE mural in the Irwin Green Child Development Center at St. Vincent de Paul Hospital in Nazareth, Israel, was completed August 21 — a four-and-a-half month collaborative project involving St. Vincent Hospital management and staff, forPEACE volunteers from Israel and the United States, and a team of dedicated artists from Idaho and Utah.
