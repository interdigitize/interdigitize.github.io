Unravel the Code

Mar 1, 2013   |  Tags: photography prototyping

Unravel the Code was the theme of an international collaboration wearable technology class and artist residency between the Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA) in Baltimore Fall 2012, and the Willem de Kooning Academy (WdKA) in Rotterdam, The Netherlands.

The course revolved around these questions:

  • Is there such a thing as an "information identity" and what are its consequences?
  • How can we make the information flow that is encircling our bodies visible?...and can we change behavior by doing so?

Students (including me worked on projects for a culminating collaborative exhibition. Following the exhibition, a catalogue was published to showcase student work and the research and learning process. The book includes several photos taken by me.

It was an amazing class organized by Annet Couwenberg and Aldje Van Meer!
