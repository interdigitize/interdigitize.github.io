Symmetrica

Jan 10, 2013   |  Tags: photography

Symmetrica is based on Rorschach ink blot tests. Designer Richard Blake developed five laser cut headpieces to examine the fusion of fashion, graphic design, and psychology. Symmetrica aims to highlight human features through dimensional wearable sculptures and emphasize awareness to the individual guise.

I photographed the pieces for Richard. The photos were selected to be included in this book, Making a Splash: Graphics that Flow.
