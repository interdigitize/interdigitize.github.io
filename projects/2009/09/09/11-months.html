Sep 9, 2009   |  Tags: audio

Never Stop Failing, a hip-hip group from Provo, Utah, featuring Jeff Wood and Chad Mustard, invited me to sing on a couple tracks for an album in 2009.

One of the songs, 11 Months, was featured in the hit XBOX Live Arcade game Shadow Complex. Check it out.
