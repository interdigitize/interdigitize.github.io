Anywhere You Go – NERVO feat. Timmy Trumpet Remix

Jan 7, 2017   |  Tags: audio

I put together this ethereal mix with an eerie edge for a Splice remix contest. I kept the atmospheric vibe of the Nervo original, just restructured it a bit and played with layers, entrances and timing—like changing the intro and outro, shifting Timmy’s fantastic trumpet serenade off the down beat (1:40), and adding rhythmic flavor to percussive elements as they re-enter after the music reduces to a sonar-like ping (2:40).

I also added synths throughout for sound effects, like data and warp speed, and used them to densify the atmosphere—and I threw in a few more vocals and lyrics for variety.
