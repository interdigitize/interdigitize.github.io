Learning about biological pest control with GAIA

Mar 29, 2013


GAIA (Global Awareness, Investigation and Action) Environmental Youth Collaboration Program completed its eight-week agenda with a four-day investigative trip to the Galilee and Negev in Israel. The trip focus was on scientific discovery regarding biological pest control. Participants included students from five schools in Israel as well two partner schools from the Czech Republic. Leading the trip was Dr. Stuart Fleischer, Director of Project GAIA and Dr. Motti Charter of Haifa University.

A classmate and I received a research grant from Maryland Institute College of Art to attend, observe and learn about the collaborative structure and hands-on teaching methodologies as well as document the trip and create summary posters and videos.

Work by a cross-diciplinary artists.
