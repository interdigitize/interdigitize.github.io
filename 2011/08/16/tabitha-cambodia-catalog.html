Tabitha Silk Products Catalog

Aug 16, 2011

The Tabitha Foundation focuses on community development and cottage industry in Cambodia. Financial sustainability is derived partly from silk weaving cottage industry. Silk sales pay for 80% of all administrative costs. Working in Tabithaâ€™s cottage industry, women develop job skills and are paid fair wages allowing them to take care of themselves and their families.

When I learned about Tabitha and wanted to purchase products, I had a hard time knowing what the products looked like from the photos in the reference catalog. So while in Phnom Penh on a forPEACE service-learning trip, I pulled together a couple photoshoots at their store and a local hotel and recruited a couple team members as models. Then took supplementary shots when I returned and put together this catalog for them.
