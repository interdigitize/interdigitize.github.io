Tabitha Silk Catalog

Apr 14, 2011



The Tabitha Foundation focuses on community development and cottage industry in Cambodia. Financial sustainability is derived partly from silk weaving cottage industry. Silk sales pay for 80% of all administrative costs. Working in Tabitha’s cottage industry, women develop job skills and are paid fair wages allowing them to take care of themselves and their families.

When Kamie learned about Tabitha and wanted to purchase products, she had a hard time knowing what the products looked like from the photos in the reference catalog. Talking with others she realized she wasn’t alone. While in Phnom Penh on a forPEACE service-learning trip, she arranged photoshoots at their store and a local hotel and recruited team members to model, then took supplementary shots when she returned home. This catalog is the result.
